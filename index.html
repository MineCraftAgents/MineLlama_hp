MineLlama

Abstract

MineLlama is a lightweight framework that utilizes a localized large language model (LLM), Llama2, to enhance decision-making in the sandbox game Minecraft, without relying on external APIs. MineLlama operates a two-layer framework, consisting of planning and executing modules. In the planning module, it employs a retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) paired with a query engine that utilizes recipe information from Minecraft to decompose a final goal into a series of interdependent subgoals. In the executing module, MineLlama is also constructed using a RAG, with a query engine informed by general knowledge of Minecraft, to guide the agent in choosing the appropriate actions.
